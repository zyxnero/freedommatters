Tripping Alone

May 06,2024

I do not know the destination, but at least I am on the way. 我不知道未來怎樣，但至少我已在路上。

Facing ahead, there's no direction, 前方啊 沒有方向

On my body, there's no clothing. 身上啊 沒有了衣裳

Blood is seeping from my wings, 鮮血啊 滲出了翅膀

Tear is wetting my chest. 我的眼淚 濕透了胸膛

Flying, enduring the pain, 飛翔著 強忍著傷

I escaped from hunters' gun. 逃離了 獵人的槍

Dear mother, beloved friends, 親愛的母親 摯愛的朋友

I will stay strong. 我會堅定 好好的活

Even with everlasting wounds, 縱然帶著永遠的傷口

At least I still possess freedom. 至少我還擁有自由
